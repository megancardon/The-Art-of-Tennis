The Art of Tennis

Types of Strokes

  1. Forehand
    • A forehand is typically executed with your dominant arm
    • It includes a takeback, swing, and follow-through

  2. Backhand
    • A backhand is usually executed with two hands
    • You swing on your non-dominant hand's side
    • It includes adjusting your grip, a takeback, swing, and follow-through

  3. Volley
    • A volley happens when you are up at the net
    • You don't wait for the ball to bounce, you hit it out of the air
    • You want to fully extend your arm in the direction you want the ball to go

  4. Serve
    • A serve is the most important stroke in tennis because it gets the point going
    • There are multiple kinds of serves with the three most common being a flat serve, slice serve, and kick serve
    • The physical serve includes a toss, a backswing, and a finish
